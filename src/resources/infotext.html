• Colors

    • Speed
      The colour of a car ranges from green to red. Red cars are stationary while green cars drive with the max. allowed speed.

    • Behaviour
      Reckless drivers will be coloured in red, cautious drivers in light blue and semi drivers in orange.

  • Parameters

    • Spawn Delay
      With a spawn delay of n, a new vehicle will be spawned at an arbitrary entrypoint every n ticks.

    • Car / Reckless Driver Percentage
      The percentage of cars/reckless drivers in relation to trucks/cautious drivers.

    • Randomise Trafficlights
      Will assing a random phase time between 3 and 6 seconds to all intersections on the map.
      Double click on an intersection to adjust the traffic light phases individually.

  • Statistics

    • Congestion
      The congestion represents the percentage of currently occupied road tiles.

    • Avg Waiting Time
      The red line shows the average seconds (in real-time) that a vehicle is idle on its way through the map. The orange line shows represents the idle time of ambulances.
